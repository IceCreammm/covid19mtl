Coronavirus COVID-19

About COVID-19

If you are worried about COVID-19 or if you have flu-like symptoms or gastroenteritis symptoms or similar to COVID-19, you can call 514-644-4545 and 1-877-644-4545 (toll free) elsewhere in Québec. For the hearing impaired (TTY), call 1-800-361 9596 (toll free). See the information on COVID-19 in Québec:  Quebec.ca/coronavirus
